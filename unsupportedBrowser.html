For Internet Explorer users

The WSDOT GeoPortal no longer works in Internet Explorer 11. We understand we have customers who may not have a choice to use a different browser. We are working to support Internet Explorer 11 again.

Please note that Microsoft has discontinued support for versions of Internet Explorer older than 11. They recommend customers upgrade to Windows 10 and the Microsoft Edge browser. If you are working in a managed computer environment please work with your IT support group to install a browser from the list.

If you have further questions you may contact us by email.
