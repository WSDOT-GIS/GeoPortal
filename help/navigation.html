Map Navigation

You can zoom and pan the map in the following ways.

  • Drag the mouse to pan
  • Mouse Scroll Forward to zoom in
  • Mouse Scroll Backward to zoom out
  • SHIFT + Drag the mouse to zoom in
  • SHIFT + CTRL + Drag the mouse to zoom out
  • SHIFT + Click to recenter
  • Double Click to Center and Zoom in
  • SHIFT + Double Click to Center and Zoom in
  • Use arrow keys to pan
  • Use + key to zoom in a level
  • Use - key to zoom out a level

Zoom Controls

  • Zoom in and out using the zoom slider on the left side of the map.
  • The toolbar in the upper-right corner of the map contains the following controls
    • Zoom to full extent
    • Zoom to previous extent (Only available if there is a previous extent to return to.)
    • Zoom to next extent (Only available if you have previously used the Zoom to previous extent button.)

Zoom to specific areas

To access these controls, click on the Tools tab and then the Zoom Controls header within that tab.

Zoom to my current location

Clicking the Zoom to my current location button will use the W3C Geolocation API. to determine your current location.

Zoom to County, City, or Urban Area

You can zoom to a county, city or urban area using the corresponding text box.

  1. Begin typing into the text box.
  2. A list of items will appear in the box. You can keep typing to further narrow down the results.
  3. Use the up and down arrow keys to select on of the items from the list.
  4. Press enter. The map will zoom to the selected location.
