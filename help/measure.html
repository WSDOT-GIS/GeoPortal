Measure

There are three measurement tools: Area, Distance and Location.

Area

  1. Click the Area button.
  2. Move the cursor to the map.
  3. Click on the map to start drawing a polygon.
  4. Click on additional locations on the map to add vertices to the polygon.
  5. Double-click on the map to close the polygon and stop drawing.
  6. You will now see the area in the Measurement Result section. You can change the measurement units at this point if you wish.
  7. When you are done using the Area tool, click the Area button again to turn it off.

Distance

  1. Click the Distance button.
  2. Move the cursor to the map.
  3. Click on the map to start drawing a polyline.
  4. Click on additional locations on the map to add vertices to the polyline.
  5. Double-click on the map to stop drawing the polyline.
  6. You will now see the distance in the Measurement Result section. You can change the measurement units at this point if you wish.
  7. When you are done using the Measure tool, click the Measure button again to turn it off.

Location

  1. Click the Location button.
  2. As you move the mouse cursor along the map, the Longitude and Latitude will be displayed.
  3. Click a location on the map to get the location of that point and turn off the measure tool.
